About SUN-X

Building A Solid Future

SUN-X Group is an established Concrete Supplier in Chennai with a strong foothold in mining and Ready-Mix Concrete. Powered by unique technology and state-of-the-art equipment, we are well-equipped to handle all construction-related materials, be it — Ready-Mix, aggregate, cement and allied materials.

Vision

To achieve leadership position in the industry and shape the way the future is built. We are committed to maintain highest levels of principles for our employees in regards to health, safety and the environment while promoting leadership and cognizance.

Mission

Driven by trust, transparency and a ceaseless commitment to quality, we are passionate about delivering unparalleled and sustainable solutions in concrete, construction, mining and allied services. We pride ourselves on creating unmatched value for all stakeholders.
