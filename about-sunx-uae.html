Who We Are

SUN-X UAE

SUN-X UAE is the international arm of the SUN-X Group — one of South India's most established concrete and construction materials companies with over 25 years of industry experience. As we expand our footprint to the UAE, we bring the same uncompromising commitment to quality, trust, and operational excellence that has made us a preferred partner for hundreds of construction projects across Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh.

In the UAE, we are positioned to serve the rapidly growing demand for high-quality Ready-Mix Concrete, aggregates, and allied building materials. Our entry into this market is backed by state-of-the-art technology, a proven supply chain, and a team with deep technical expertise in concrete production and logistics.
