Every batch tested. Every delivery certified. Our multi-stage quality control process ensures that SUN-X concrete meets — and exceeds — Indian Standard specifications on every pour.

Our Commitment

Built on Precision,
Delivered with Confidence

At SUN-X, quality assurance is not a checkpoint — it is embedded into every stage of production. From raw material selection to final discharge, our QA engineers monitor mix design parameters, aggregate grading, water-cement ratios, and admixture dosage with meticulous accuracy.

Our on-site laboratories are equipped with compression testing machines, slump-flow apparatus, and fresh-concrete analysis tools that allow real-time decision-making before any truck leaves the plant.

We follow IS:456, IS:10262, and project-specific mix design briefs endorsed by structural consultants — so every cubic metre dispatched carries the full confidence of a traceable quality record.

How We Do It

Our QA Process

01
🧪

Raw Material Inspection

Every incoming aggregate, cement and admixture shipment is sampled and tested for gradation, specific gravity, moisture content and deleterious matter before being accepted into the plant.

02
⚗️

Mix Design Validation

Mix designs are prepared to IS:10262 and verified with trial mixes before production. Target mean strength accounts for standard deviation history from our own plant records.

03
🔬

Fresh Concrete Testing

Slump, temperature, air content, and unit weight are measured on every truck at departure. Any non-conforming batch is rejected before dispatch — not after delivery.

04
🏗️

Cube Sampling & Curing

Cube samples are cast, cured and tested at 7-day and 28-day intervals. Results are logged against each batch ticket and shared with the client's site engineer on request.

05
📋

Delivery Documentation

Each delivery comes with a batch ticket showing mix code, design strength, w/c ratio, admixture type, plant departure time, and the name of the QA engineer who signed off the batch.

06
📊

Statistical Analysis & Review

Monthly QA reports track characteristic strength trends, coefficient of variation, and non-conformance rates. Data feeds back into mix design refinements to continually raise our performance baseline.

Standards We Uphold

Our quality management system is aligned with recognised Indian and international construction standards — ensuring your project meets statutory, structural and contractual requirements.

  • IS:456 — Plain & Reinforced ConcreteCode of practice for the use of structural concrete in all exposure conditions.
  • IS:10262 — Concrete Mix DesignGuidelines for proportioning concrete mixes to achieve specified strength and durability.
  • IS:516 — Cube TestingMethods of testing compressive strength of concrete specimens at 7 and 28 days.
  • IS:383 — Coarse & Fine AggregatesSpecifications for natural aggregates used in concrete, including grading and impurity limits.
  • IS:9103 — Admixtures for ConcreteSpecification for chemical admixtures ensuring compatibility with cement and mix stability.

Require a Quality Plan for Your Project?

Our QA engineers can prepare a project-specific quality control plan, including testing frequency, cube sampling schedules and reporting formats aligned with your consultant's requirements.
