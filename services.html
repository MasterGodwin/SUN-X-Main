Quality Assurance

Quality In Every Batch

We implement a comprehensive quality management system to ensure consistent, reliable and specification-compliant concrete for every project. Our production and testing procedures comply with BS, BS EN 206, ASTM, ACI and Dubai Municipality requirements.

  • Approved & tested raw materials
  • Laboratory trial mixes & mix design
  • Automated, calibrated batching
  • Continuous temperature control
  • Fresh concrete testing at plant & site
  • Full batch traceability & documentation
