Our Infrastructure

Plants Built for
Scale & Precision

SUN-X operates a network of fully automated RMC plants positioned across key urban and industrial corridors in Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh. Each facility is engineered to deliver consistent, high-quality concrete with minimal batch-to-batch variation.

Our plants feature computerised batching systems with load cell accuracy of ±0.5%, twin-shaft mixers for homogeneous blending, and covered aggregate storage to eliminate moisture variation — the most common cause of strength inconsistency in site-mixed concrete.

With multiple plants, we can serve concurrent large-scale pours across different sites without compromising turnaround time or mix quality.

Plant Capabilities

What Each Facility Offers

01
🖥️

Computerised Batching

Fully automated batching control with load cell weighing accuracy of ±0.5% for cement, aggregates, water and admixtures — eliminating human weighing error.

02
🔄

Twin-Shaft Mixing

High-intensity twin-shaft mixers ensure thorough, homogeneous blending of all mix components within 60–90 seconds, delivering consistent workability on every batch.

03
🏭

Covered Aggregate Storage

Enclosed aggregate bins prevent rain-induced moisture variation — the primary cause of strength inconsistency. Moisture probes enable real-time water correction.

04
🌡️

Chilled Water System

Select plants are equipped with ice-flake and chilled-water systems for temperature-controlled concrete production — critical for mass pours and hot-weather concreting.

05
🗄️

Silo & Bin Management

Multi-compartment cement silos and aggregate bins allow seamless grade switching between jobs without contamination — essential for sites running multiple concrete grades concurrently.

06
📡

Remote Monitoring

Plant supervisors and dispatch managers can monitor batch weights, mixer load, and truck positions in real time via our integrated plant management software.
