BATCHING PLANT

Precision-automated batching technology that weighs, blends and delivers consistent Ready-Mix Concrete — removing variability from the equation and putting control in the hands of our engineers.

Technology

Precision at Every
Stage of Batching

The batching plant is the heart of a Ready-Mix Concrete operation. At SUN-X, our plants use fully computerised batching control systems that weigh cement, aggregates, water and admixtures to within ±0.5% of the target — eliminating the variability inherent in manual weighing.

Twin-shaft compulsory mixers provide intensive blending in 60–90 seconds, producing a homogeneous mix that is discharged directly into the transit mixer drum — with no re-mixing required on transit.

Our batching software logs every parameter of every batch, creating a traceable production record tied to the delivery ticket, cube sample reference and QA sign-off for full auditability.

Plant Components

Inside Our Batching System

01
⚖️

Load Cell Weighing

Digitally-calibrated load cells on all aggregate bins and cement silos provide continuous, real-time weight readings — corrected automatically for moisture content measured by in-bin probes.

02
🔄

Twin-Shaft Mixer

Counter-rotating paddles achieve thorough blending across the full mix volume within 90 seconds — far superior to drum or pan mixers for complex high-performance concrete grades.

03
💧

Chilled Water System

Chilled water and ice-flake systems at select plants maintain fresh concrete temperature below 30°C — critical for summer concreting, mass pours and temperature-sensitive grades.

04
🧴

Admixture Dosing

Automated liquid admixture dispensers dose plasticisers, retarders, accelerators and other additives to within ±1% of target, ensuring consistent workability and setting behaviour.

05
🖥️

SCADA Control Panel

A single operator workstation controls all batching sequences, monitors live weights, stores mix designs and flags out-of-tolerance conditions before the batch is discharged.

06
📝

Batch Ticket Printing

Every load automatically generates a printed batch ticket showing date, time, mix design code, target and actual quantities, truck number and QA technician name — for full traceability.

Production Sequence

From Silo to Truck
in 90 Seconds

01

Mix Design Recall

The operator selects the project mix code. The SCADA system retrieves target weights for all materials, including the real-time moisture correction values from the aggregate probes.

02

Aggregate Weighing

Coarse and fine aggregates are drawn from their bins onto the weigh conveyor in sequence — each weighed to ±0.5% accuracy before being transferred to the mixer.

03

Cement & Additions

Cement and supplementary cementitious materials (fly ash, GGBFS) are weighed simultaneously from their dedicated silos and discharged to the mixer on confirmation.

04

Water & Admixture Addition

Weighed water and dosed liquid admixtures are introduced to the mixer. Water quantity is adjusted in real time based on aggregate moisture probe readings to hit the target w/c ratio.

05

Twin-Shaft Mixing

Counter-rotating paddles blend all materials for 60–90 seconds until the mixer motor load sensor confirms homogeneous mix consistency.

06

Discharge & Dispatch

The batch is discharged into the transit mixer drum, the batch ticket is printed, QA fresh testing is performed, and the truck is dispatched with a time-stamp on the delivery system.
