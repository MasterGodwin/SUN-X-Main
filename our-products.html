OUR PRODUCTS

A wide range of specialised concrete solutions engineered for performance, durability and every application — backed by SUN-X quality and expertise.

Temperature Controlled Concrete

Mass Concrete High Durability

Mixed concrete with lower heat of hydration for mass concrete requirements — ensuring lower core temperature and lower temperature differentials, and subsequently higher durability.

Applications
  • Raft foundations
  • Large footings
  • Dams
  • Thick retaining walls
Properties
  • Lower drying stress
  • Lower temperature stress
  • Higher durability

02 · Self Compacting

Self Compacting Concrete

SCC No Vibration

Fresh concrete that flows under its own weight — no external vibration required. Ideal for complex reinforcement configurations and hard-to-reach areas.

Applications
  • Drilled shafts
  • Columns
  • Earth retaining systems
  • High rebar areas
Properties
  • Higher strength vs traditional
  • Resistance to segregation
  • No vibration needed

03 · Green Building

Green Building Concrete

Sustainable Low CO₂

Extra steps in mix design and placement to ensure a sustainable structure, long life cycle, and low maintenance surface — with energy saving and reduced CO₂ emissions.

Applications
  • Green homes
  • Exterior walls
  • Pavements
Properties
  • CO₂ reduced ≥ 30%
  • Min 20% residual aggregate
  • CO₂-neutral fuels ≥ 10%
HDD
04 · High Density
04 · High Density

High Density Concrete

Radiation Shield 5200 kg/m³

Density ~50% more than conventional concrete — up to 5,200 kg/m³ using iron aggregate. Absorbs neutron and gamma rays, reducing radiation to a very weak state.

Applications
  • Nuclear reactor structures
  • Radiation control buildings
  • EM wave shielding
Properties
  • Absorbs neutron & gamma rays
  • Good mechanical strength
  • High durability
HSC
05 · High Strength
05 · High Strength

High Strength Concrete

200 MPa High Rise

Formulated with portland cement, reactive powders, quartz flour, fine sand, and high-range water reducers. Achieves compressive strengths exceeding 29,000 psi (200 MPa).

Applications
  • Highway bridges
  • Concrete girders
  • Buildings over 30 storeys
Properties
  • Compressive strength > 200 MPa
  • Dense, smooth surface finish
  • Precise form-detail transfer

06 · Early Strength
06 · Early Strength

Early Strength Concrete

>60 MPa at 28d High Performance

High compressive strength (>60 MPa) after 28 days — classified in the high performance group. Versatile technical characteristics make it ideal for earthquake-zone high-rise structures.

Applications
  • High load-bearing columns
  • Precast plant products
  • High-rise earthquake-zone buildings
Properties
  • Higher cement content
  • Reduced water-cement ratio
  • Better workability & compaction

07 · Light Weight

Light Weight Concrete

Thermal Fire Resistant

Significantly reduces dead loads, making savings in foundations and reinforcement. Improved thermal properties and fire resistance — with equivalent structural strengths where required.

Applications
  • Structural applications
  • Architectural covering
  • Roof heat insulation
  • Water pipe insulation
Properties
  • Reduced dead loads
  • Improved thermal properties
  • Improved fire resistance
  • Less formwork & propping

Properties
  • High porosity structure
  • Direct water pass-through
  • Reduces runoff & flooding
